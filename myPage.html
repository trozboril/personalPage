Bio...

I was born in Naperville, IL which is a suburb of chicago. I enjoyed hanging out with friends, playing sports, and submersing myself in video game environments.

I played water polo, and swam for my highschool. I wasn't amazing at either. I was not a popular guy, but wasn't an outcast. It was more like I flew under the radar most of my higschool years.

I went to college and started acting like a person I was not. Luckily, I was smart enough to not continue down that road, and joined the USAF. I got honorably discharged, and started going to school for computer engineering. My wife got pregnant and now I find myself in Denver going to Galvanize for web development.
